About This Blog

Hello and thank you for visiting My Buffalo Bills Addiction. This page is dedicated to the Buffalo Bills fans who just need a little more Bills Mafia in their lives. This blog will be providing you with a daily analysis of what's going on with our favorite players and coaches, potential prospects, game and practice evalutations, and much more! We recently signed Von Miller and signed Stefon Diggs to an incredible 4 year contract and can't wait to see what else is in store for us before the season starts! Josh is looking healthy and couldn't be anymore excited for the season starts and know our Mafia members are feeling the same energy! Don't forget to pick up some Labbatt's and a couple of party tables to break once the season gets going.
